# Catalyst Zero Research

Independent research organization focused on materials science, scientific machine learning, and AI-driven computational research.

The goal is to build systems that help scientific discovery move faster: less isolated PDF digging, more connected reasoning across structures, properties, synthesis pathways, datasets, and research literature.

## Current Work

| Project | What it is |
|---|---|
| [TRIADS](https://github.com/Catalyst-Zero-Research/TRIADS) | Fork/copy of TRIADS, a recursive neural architecture for materials-property prediction and related materials ML workflows. |

## Research Direction

- Materials-science knowledge graphs
- Scientific machine learning
- Materials-property prediction
- AI-native research systems
- Literature, datasets, and structure/property reasoning
